[ Follow the Earthbeat ! ]

One or two players can push the 'beat' buttons on the keyboard. [ player 1 : S for yellow , D for red ; player 2 : K for yellow , L for red ]

If done in rhythm with the heartbeat of the Planet, the familly will feel the path to take and walk accordingly.
